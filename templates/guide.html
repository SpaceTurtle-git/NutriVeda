The Science of Life

Ayurveda
A Guide to Balance

Ayurveda, the 5,000-year-old "science of life," teaches that health is harmony between body, mind, and spirit. Your unique constitution (Prakriti) is defined by the three doshas.

🍂 Air + Space

Vata

Qualities: Dry, light, cold, rough, subtle, mobile.

Balanced: Creative, energetic, flexible.
Imbalanced: Anxiety, insomnia, constipation, joint pain.

Pacify with:

Warm, moist, grounding foods; routine; oil massage.

🔥 Fire + Water

Pitta

Qualities: Hot, sharp, light, oily, liquid, spreading.

Balanced: Intelligent, driven, good digestion.
Imbalanced: Heartburn, anger, inflammation, rashes.

Pacify with:

Cool, refreshing, non-spicy foods; avoid midday heat.

🌊 Earth + Water

Kapha

Qualities: Heavy, slow, cool, oily, smooth, stable.

Balanced: Calm, loving, strong immunity.
Imbalanced: Weight gain, lethargy, congestion, attachment.

Pacify with:

Light, dry, warm, spicy foods; regular exercise.

Ayurvedic Food Pyramid

Eat Least: Heavy, Oily, Cold Foods
Occasional: Fermented, Sour, Salty
Daily: Whole Grains, Veggies, Legumes
Foundation: Fresh Fruits, Herbal Teas

* Portion size and food choices vary by dosha. Consult a practitioner for personalisation.

🌅 Dinacharya (Daily Routine)

Morning: Wake before sunrise, scrape tongue, oil pulling, warm water, elimination, exercise.
Midday: Largest meal at noon when digestive fire is strongest. Sit quietly after eating.
Evening: Light dinner, self-massage (abhyanga), early to bed by 10pm.
